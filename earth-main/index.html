Explore Earth

  • Position in Solar System: Earth is the third planet from the Sun.
  • Unique for Life: The only known planet that supports life.
  • Surface Composition: ~71% covered by water (oceans, seas, lakes, rivers).
  • Continents & Landforms: Contains mountains, plains, deserts, valleys, and forests.
